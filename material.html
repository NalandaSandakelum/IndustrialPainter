  • What is Sanding Sealer

    Use of Sandon Sealer

    Sanding sealer is not a compulsory product when it comes to wood finishing. The main purpose of a sanding sealer is to save unnecessary wastage of varnish, top coat or stain by sealing off the wood surface. The main reason for this is that certain wood types can be so porous and absorptive they might waste a lot of finishing or staining coatings. Typically all bare wooden surfaces are highly absorptive. In this, soft wood surfaces are highly absorptive and hard wood surfaces are less absorptive.

  • Sand Paper Guide

    Choosing the right sandpaper

    Sand papers are usually numbered according to its grit size (sand Particle). Small sandpaper numbers are made of larger grits, and are suitable for coarser applications. Larger sand paper numbers are made of smaller grits, and are suitable for smoother applications.

    Common sand paper sizes (numbers) range from 40 to 400 (Sizes beyond this range are usually used for automotive applications).
